# Insurance Claims SQL Analysis
# 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes insurance claims data using SQL to solve real-world business problems related to claim processing, approval efficiency, fraud detection, policy performance, and regional trends.

The analysis consists of 45 business-driven SQL queries covering data quality checks, KPI calculations, advanced analytics, and performance reporting.

# Objectives
- Validate data quality and consistency
- Analyze claim approval and rejection patterns
- Measure claim processing efficiency
- Identify potential fraudulent claims
- Evaluate policy and regional performance
- Track monthly claim trends and growth

# Business Insights Generated

1. The West region contributes the highest percentage of total claim value.

2. Cancer and Cardiac Surgery diagnoses account for the largest payouts.

3. Premium policies demonstrate the highest approval efficiency.

4. A small group of hospitals accounts for a significant share of total claims.

5. Several claims exceed the statistical fraud threshold and require investigation.

6. Deductions represent a meaningful portion of claim value and vary across policy types.

7. Claim volume shows month-over-month growth trends that can be monitored for forecasting.

Developed a comprehensive SQL-based insurance claims analysis solution capable of generating actionable business insights, monitoring operational performance, and supporting data-driven decision-making.
